How to Fix Your TCX Files and Upload to Strava

Follow these steps to clean up your .tcx files from Garmin and upload them to Strava:

1. Download Your TCX File from Garmin

  • Open your Activities page in Garmin Connect.
  • Open the activity you want to export.
  • Click the gear icon ⚙️ in the top right corner, then choose Export to TCX.

2. Use the Tool to Clean or Merge Files

  • To fill gaps in a TCX file, click the Fill Gaps link above.
  • To merge multiple TCX files, click the Join Files link above.

Once on the tool page:

  • Upload your .tcx file(s).
  • Use the tool to fix your file(s).
  • Download the modified .tcx output file.

3. Upload to Strava

  • Go to Strava Upload.
  • Choose File and upload your modified .tcx file.
  • Save the activity and verify everything looks good!
